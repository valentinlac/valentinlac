Who is Valentin ?

Valentin is a content creator based in France who wants to make his life a real story, a real adventure with ever greater and limitless goals.

And one of his goals is, first and foremost, to break out of a society and a set framework. For me, life is a magnificent thing with no real second chance. And so I think that spending your life in front of a desk working under someone else's orders is not what life is all about.

So to avoid that, I decided to create my own personal brand. It will be the driving force behind all my projects, which I'll let you see below.

For me, work is the only thing that separates us from our dreams and objectives. I've become addicted to work, addicted to the process. The only thing that attracts me is work.

For me, money is just a tool to help us have experiences. And that's not what life is all about. Life is all about relationships, the 5 senses and lots of other things that money can't buy.

His ambitions

01

The right to real freedom

Ever since I started school, I've hated being told what to do, and even more so when there's no point. But for me being free isn't just that, being free also means being able to go where I want when I want without having to worry about the money in my bank account. For me, freedom is being able to do what I want with the people I love without having any real big restrictions.

02

Creating my own interior design brand

I've always loved creating. And when I was little, I always wanted to be an architect so that I could be master of my surroundings. And so I've always kept in the back of my mind the idea of wanting to create something that represents our personality and our way of life, and what better place to do that than the place we live in. So in a few years' time, I hope to be able to become part of your personality through my art in interior design.

03

My ultimate project

As I said, I love creating, creating lots of things in lots of different areas. And my final project would be to extend this creativity through different products of different types and in different areas. So my aim would be to keep an exclusive product in each collection to really emphasise the idea of community in this project. There are still a lot of things to define, but this project is one of the things that drives me the most and I'll do anything to achieve it.
